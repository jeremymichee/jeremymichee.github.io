About Me

My name is Jeremy Michée, I’m 37 years old, and I’m currently retraining in computer science. I’m studying for a BTS SIO, SLAM at the CFA of the Faculté des Métiers de l'Essonne in Massy, where I’m learning to build applications and develop software.

For two years, I worked at TUMO as a coach for students, helping them develop their skills in programming. This experience allowed me to enhance my knowledge — especially in JavaScript.

Alongside this, I taught myself HTML and CSS, and I earned a FreeCodeCamp certification in Responsive Web Design.

I’m also passionate about drawing and music production (MAO), both of which I learned on my own. These creative fields inspire me to combine art and technology in everything I do.

My goal is to become an AI engineer, and to create innovative projects at the crossroads of code, creativity, and human expression.
